About Us

Police Feedback System (PFS) is a web application that helps police officers to report crime and incidents to the police department.

PURPOSE

Provide residents a direct channel to give input about police services.

Confidentiality

Feedback can be submitted anonymously for those concerned about identification.

Categories

Complaints, compliments, suggestions, and crime reports can all be shared.

Processing

Qualified staff objectively review and analyze all feedback for follow-up action.

Impact

Constructive feedback enables continuous improvement and policy reforms.
